# Medical Reminder App 

An essential and reliable personal healthcare assistant designed to help users maintain their health by ensuring timely medication adherence. This application addresses the critical issue of forgotten medicine schedules, which often leads to incomplete courses and potential health risks.

*   **Timely Notifications:** Sends accurate, time-bound reminders to ensure you never miss your medication.
*   **Adherence Tracking:** Provides a detailed overview of total adherence, missed doses, and taken doses in a comprehensive table format.
*   **Health Alerts:** Automatically generates alerts (e.g., "Advice to Doctor") if missed doses exceed a specified threshold (e.g., two or more), promoting proactive health management.
*   **User-Centric Design:** Simple and intuitive interface for easy scheduling and tracking of multiple medicines.

##  Motivation

Incomplete medication courses due to forgetfulness can severely impact health and often lead to avoidable complications. This app serves as a crucial bridge between patients and their health regimen, ensuring accountability and supporting better health outcomes.
